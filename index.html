Our story

Designed for homes that tell stories.

Devilixirs started as a tiny studio shelf — a place to gather objects that felt warm, intentional and made with care. Today, we work with independent makers to bring you pieces that honour craft, culture and the slow joy of living at home.

Every product is made in small batches, using materials chosen for durability and touch. No mass production, no throwaway trends — just timeless silhouettes that age beautifully with you.

  • Small-batch, handcrafted pieces
  • Fair pricing for artisans
  • Thoughtful, low-waste packaging
  • Designed in India, loved worldwide
