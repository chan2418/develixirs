Hair ritual

Turn hair oiling into a 10-minute reset.

Our herbal oils are designed to work with your existing routine. No overnight sticky mess, no harsh fragrance — just a calm, grounding ritual your scalp will look forward to.

1
Warm 2–3 spoons of oil.

Pour into your palm or a small bowl and warm gently. The warmth helps herbs penetrate faster without making hair greasy.

2
Massage roots for 5 minutes.

Use your fingertips to massage in circular motions. Focus on crown and hairline to boost blood circulation.

3
Keep for 45–60 minutes.

Leave it on before a mild shampoo. For intense repair, use overnight once a week with a soft towel on your pillow.

Use 2–3 times a week for at least 8 weeks for visible reduction in hair fall and improved shine.
