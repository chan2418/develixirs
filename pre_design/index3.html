Ritual

Hair oil · Cleanse · Aloe – your 3-step Devilixirs ritual.

Herbal doesn’t have to be messy and time-consuming. We built a simple ritual that fits weekday life and still gives real results.

1
Warm & massage hair oil.

Take 2–3 tsp of hair oil, warm gently and massage into scalp for 5 minutes using fingertips – focus on crown, hairline and back of neck.

2
Cleanse with a mild face wash.

Use Neem or Saffron cleanser to remove sweat, pollution and sunscreen without stripping natural moisture.

3
Seal with aloe gel.

Apply a thin layer of aloe gel on damp skin or scalp to calm irritation, lock in hydration and add light, non-sticky comfort.

Use 2–3 times a week for 8–12 weeks for visible reduction in hair fall, calmer skin and softer texture.
